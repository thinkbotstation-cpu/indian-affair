Award-Winning Indian Dining

Indian Affair Restaurant & Bar has been delighting locals and visitors in Taupo with authentic Indian cuisine. Our chefs craft every dish using traditional recipes passed down through generations, blending aromatic spices ground fresh in-house with the finest local ingredients. Whether you're craving a rich Butter Chicken, crispy Mo:Mos, or our famous Googley Special, there's something for everyone.
