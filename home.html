Our Story

Real Food, Real Atmosphere

Indian Affair is Taupo's favourite restobar — relaxed enough for a Tuesday night, special enough for a celebration. We cook with spices ground in-house, recipes passed down through generations, and a bar menu built to match. Pull up a chair, order a round, and stay a while.
