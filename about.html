Our Story

Taupo's Award Winning Indian Dining Experience

Who We Are

Indian Affair Restaurant & Bar is an award-winning Indian dining destination located in the heart of Taupo, New Zealand, at the corner of Tuwharetoa and Ruapehu Streets. We bring the rich and diverse flavours of the Indian subcontinent right to your table — from the creamy curries of North India to the aromatic dosas of the South, and the hearty Balti dishes of Nepal.

Our Cuisine

Our chefs draw on authentic recipes passed down through generations, using traditional techniques including our prized tandoor oven — a clay oven that reaches extreme temperatures to create the perfect naan, succulent tikka, and smoky kebabs that keep our customers coming back. We offer a diverse mix of traditional and modern Indian cuisine, ensuring there's something for every palate.

Signature Dishes

We're known for our signature dishes including the Bhindi Masala — a flavourful okra dish unique to Indian Affair — the Eggplant & Mushroom made from grandma's own recipe, and the incredibly popular Kathmandu Balti, a traditional Nepali curry that has become a Taupo favourite. Our Googley Special and Butter Chicken are legendary, and our Mo:Mos (Nepali dumplings) are unlike anything else you'll find in town.

More Than Just Dinner

Whether you're joining us for a sit-down meal, grabbing a takeaway, or ordering from our breakfast menu featuring South Indian dosas and Nepali mo:mos, we promise you an experience that's warm, generous, and unforgettable. We're also perfect for functions, birthday parties, and group bookings — let us make your celebration special.

Award-Winning Excellence

Indian Affair has been recognised for outstanding Indian dining in the Taupo region. Our commitment to authentic flavours, quality ingredients, and warm hospitality has earned us a reputation as one of the best Indian restaurants in the area.

The Atmosphere

Step into Indian Affair and you'll feel the warmth immediately. Our dining space blends modern comfort with touches of Indian heritage — warm lighting, rich colours, and the inviting aroma of spices from our open kitchen.

Whether it's an intimate dinner for two, a lively celebration with friends, or a corporate function, our space adapts to make every occasion memorable. We cater for groups large and small, with customisable menus for special events.
